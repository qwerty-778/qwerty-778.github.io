FAQ

Will I be learning new concepts?

No. At this time we are not available to advance your child's coursework. Your assigned tutor will reteach specific concepts that the child may need help with, and have them attempt the subject yourself. Please send a photo of the material you need help with (included in registration form).


Is there an age-gate to enroll?

Yes, we work with middle schoolers (6th-8th grade).


Will my family need to subscribe and visit weekly?

No, please prepay before your session and if you cannot make it on that specific date, we have a makeup day on Saturday at 3:00 pm.


What is the pricing for lessons?

Currently, the pricing for lessons is $6 per session. However, please keep in mind that the price can change and it isn’t permanent.


Are we allowed to attend more than one class a week?

Yes! We hold classes on Saturday for making up sessions or if you feel like you want to attend another meeting, you can choose what day(s)! Be sure to plan ahead. You are eligible to sign up again after your first class that week. Email us a few business days in advance!
